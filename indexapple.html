Why are they number 1?

Apples reign as the premier fruit for their crisp texture, refreshing juiciness, and versatile flavor.

Whether eaten fresh, baked, or juiced, they offer a delightful blend of sweetness and tanginess.

Packed with fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, apples promote health and longevity.

Their durability and convenience as a snack further underscore their status as the best fruit globally.
