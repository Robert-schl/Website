Why are they number 2?

Oranges rank second only to apples as one of the world's best fruits.

Their juicy sweetness, rich in vitamin C and nutrients, makes them a popular choice for both flavor and health benefits.

Whether eaten fresh, juiced, or used in recipes, oranges are cherished for their refreshing taste and nutritional value.

Their durability and convenience as a snack further underscore their status as the best fruit globally.
