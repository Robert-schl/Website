Why are they number 3?

Grapes take third place among the world's best fruits due to their delicious sweetness and versatility.

Whether fresh, dried, or turned into wine, grapes offer a delightful taste and are packed with antioxidants and vitamins, making them a healthy choice for snacks and recipes alike.

Grapes' enduring popularity across cultures underscores their well-deserved status as one of the top fruits globally.
